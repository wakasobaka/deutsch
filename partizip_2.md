**Weak verbs** *(schwache Verben)* are regular verbs; they follow a fixed pattern

| Verb   | Präteritum | Partizip 2  | Eng              |
| ------ | ---------- | ----------- | ---------------- |
| machen | mach       | hat gemacht | make, do, делать |
